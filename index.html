Wana Mama

WanaMama LLC is a private holistic Occupational Therapy practice that provides body and energy healing services as well as group gatherings, education and support for women and mothers in the Holland Michigan area. WanaMama is passionate about providing a safe, clean, therapeutic environment for healing, restoration and building a community of mothers. The services WanaMama offers are provided with a mission to promote the importance of self-care and healing associated with infertility, pregnancy loss, pelvic health, pregnancy, child-birth, post-partum issues and parenting. The mission is to empower women to take control of their health and happiness to be the best people and mothers they can be.

Who is Janelle?

Janelle has been practicing Occupational Therapy since 2007 and has explored several areas of OT from inpatient physical rehab settings, hospitals, mental and psychological health, elementary education and running her own practice for health and wellness. No matter the setting, Janelle has always incorporated complementary and alternative therapies into her practice. Since the birth of her second child, she has decided to pursue a new professional endeavor; helping women and mothers nurture their health and well-being.

Janelle was raised in Holland, Michigan. Since completing her master’s degree in Occupational Therapy from Eastern Michigan University, she has lived all over the country and traveled the world with her husband and life partner of nearly 20 years, Alex. Recently, Janelle and her family relocated back to Holland and found the timing to be perfect to pursue this very exciting venture. Janelle is a proud mama to two beautiful energetic little boys. She enjoys adventuring, traveling, music, dancing, playing pool and being outside in the fresh air doing anything fun!

Healing Therapies Explained

What is reflexology?

Reflexology is an energetic and gentle touch healing modality focusing on the hands, feet and face to access the systems of the body, body structures and tissues to promote energetic balance, healing from within and relaxation. The client will be clothed throughout treatment.

What is reiki?

Reiki an energetic healing method using gentle touch on or close to the body with a focus on chakra clearing, balance and relaxation. Reiki can be performed on areas of restriction, disease or injury throughout the body. The client will be clothed throughout treatment.

What is Occupational Therapy (OT)?

OT’s help their clients find function, balance and satisfaction in life. An OT focuses on physical, social and emotional well-being. Every person is completely unique and OT is a holistic therapeutic approach to health and wellness. An OT can help individuals engage in whatever matters most to them and meets their personal needs. For example; help moms strengthen the pelvic floor after child birth so that she can jump and play with her child or go to that jazzercise class with confidence. An OT can show her clients stretches and strengthening exercises for the chest, back and core for moms who suffer from muscle tension and poor posture after spending so much time holding and nursing their little ones in those inevitably strange positions.
